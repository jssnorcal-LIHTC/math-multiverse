Answer Key

For parent/teacher use

Part A · Fractions

1. 2/3 + 3/4 = 8/12 + 9/12 = 17/12 = 1 5/12
2. 5/6 − 1/4 = 10/12 − 3/12 = 7/12
3. 2/5 × 3/4 = 6/20 = 3/10
4. 3 ÷ 1/2 = 3 × 2 = 6
5. 3/8 + 1/4 = 3/8 + 2/8 = 5/8 eaten. Left = 1 − 5/8 = 3/8 of the pizza

Part B · Decimals & Place Value

6. 5.12
7. 8.72
8. 6.4 × 1000 = 6,400
9. 385 ÷ 100 = 3.85
10. 12 × $0.45 = $5.40

Part C · Volume

11. V = L × W × H = 5 × 3 × 4 = 60 cubic inches
12. H = V ÷ (L × W) = 60 ÷ 15 = 4 feet

Part D · Coordinate Plane

13. A rectangle (4 wide, 3 tall). Bonus credit if he says “quadrilateral” or notes the dimensions.
14. (4, 3)

Part E · Order of Operations

15. 8 + 3 × 6 ÷ 2 = 8 + 18 ÷ 2 = 8 + 9 = 17
16. 8 × 2 + 6 ÷ 3 = 16 + 2 = 18
17. 20 − 36 ÷ 9 = 20 − 4 = 16

Part F · Measurement Conversion

18. 4 × 12 = 48 inches
19. 2.5 × 1000 = 2,500 grams
20. 3 × 60 + 15 = 195 minutes

Bonus

Several solutions. Verified examples:
• 8 × 4 − 6 − 2 = 32 − 8 = 24 ✓
• 6 × 8 ÷ (4 − 2) = 48 ÷ 2 = 24 ✓
• (8 − 2) × 4 + 6 − 6 ... nope, can’t reuse digits.
• (2 + 4) × (8 − 6 + 2) ... nope, reuses 2.
Encourage him to find a third one on his own.
